How it works

Next Game priority order

1
📌 Pinned players — always get a slot, regardless of whether they just played or have been waiting.
2
Waiting players — anyone sitting out fills remaining slots. Longer wait = higher priority within this group.
3
Just played — players from the previous game fill any leftover slots, picked randomly.

Buttons on each player

  • 📌Pin — guarantees this player a slot in the very next game. The pin is consumed after one use.
  • ✕Remove — takes the player out of the current session.

Adding players

  • 🎮Paste a Steam profile URL (e.g. steamcommunity.com/id/…) or a SteamID64 to auto-import their Steam name and profile picture.
  • ✏️Or just type any name and hit Add — works without Steam too.

Reset session

  • 🔄Keeps all players in the pool but clears the game counter and resets everyone's status back to Waiting.
