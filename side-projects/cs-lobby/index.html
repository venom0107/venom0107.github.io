How it works

Next Game priority order

1
Confirmed — always get a slot, regardless of how long others have waited.
2
Queue — fills remaining slots. Longer wait = higher priority. Players from the previous game are picked randomly if slots are still open.
—
Bench — completely skipped during selection.

Player status

  • ●Confirmed — locks this player into the next game. Resets to Queue after one game.
  • ●Queue — in the rotation. Selected by wait order when slots are available.
  • ●Bench — sitting out. Won't be picked until moved back to Queue.
  • ✕Remove — removes the player entirely.

Adding players

  • 🎮Paste a Steam profile URL (e.g. steamcommunity.com/id/…) or a SteamID64 to auto-import their Steam name and profile picture.
  • ✏️Or just type any name and hit Add — works without Steam too.

Reset session

  • 🔄Keeps all players but clears the game counter and resets everyone back to Queue.
